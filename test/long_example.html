Case Study

This website itself is authored in SHPAML. I preprocess SHPAML documents into templates that Django then renders. Many of the pages on the site are fairly static in nature, but I do exercise most of Django's core features in my SHPAML documents, such as inheritance, iteration, and variable interpolation.

Most of the examples that follow are from this website, but the first one was contributed by another user. (If anyone has examples they'd like to share, please contact the mailing list.)
